About Parle Candy

Parle Candy is a popular candy brand in India that has been around for over 80 years. The brand is known for its affordable and delicious candies that come in a wide variety of flavors.

Parle Candy was first introduced in India in the 1930s and since then, the company has expanded to become one of the leading candy manufacturers in the country.
